# Executive Summary

ARK Trade Hub (ATH) is an intelligent trade automation platform designed to separate **investment decision-making** from **trade execution**.

Its mission is to execute trading operations on behalf of users while ensuring that assets always remain under the ownership and control of the customer.

ATH does **not** hold customer funds.

ATH does **not** provide brokerage services.

ATH acts as a trusted execution layer between trading strategies and supported exchanges.

---

# Vision

To become the most trusted trade automation platform for Persian-speaking investors by providing secure, transparent and intelligent trade execution while preserving complete customer ownership of assets.

---

# Mission

To automate every operational aspect of professional trading businesses so that human effort shifts from repetitive execution toward strategy, quality improvement and customer success.

---

# The Story Behind ATH

ATH was born from years of real operational experience.

The founder successfully managed hundreds of customer investment accounts manually.

Customers kept their assets inside their own brokerage accounts while granting trading access to the investment manager.

This model created three major advantages:

- Customer trust
- Operational transparency
- Legal separation of ownership and execution

As the customer base rapidly expanded, manual execution became the primary operational bottleneck.

ATH exists to eliminate this bottleneck through intelligent automation.

---

# Business Problem

Professional portfolio managers face several operational challenges:

- Manual trade execution across hundreds of customer accounts
- Repetitive operational workload
- Human errors
- Slow execution
- Difficult reporting
- Manual commission calculation
- Manual referral management

These activities consume valuable time that should instead be invested in market analysis and strategy improvement.

---

# Proposed Solution

ATH automates operational trading activities while allowing investment professionals to remain focused on decision-making.

The platform provides:

- Customer management
- Exchange connectivity
- API management
- Automated order execution
- Risk management
- Performance reporting
- Commission management
- Referral management
- Notification services
- Financial dashboards

---

# Core Philosophy

ATH is built upon one simple principle:

> Assets belong to customers.

Execution belongs to ATH.

Investment decisions belong to strategy providers.

Responsibilities remain clearly separated.

---

# Guiding Principles

## Customer Trust First

Every architectural decision must increase customer confidence.

---

## Automation Before Manual Work

If a repetitive process can safely be automated, automation is preferred.

---

## Security By Design

Security is considered during design—not added later.

---

## Modular Architecture

Every subsystem should be independently replaceable.

---

## Low Coupling

Services communicate through clearly defined interfaces.

No unnecessary dependencies are introduced.

---

## Human Oversight

Automation assists humans.

Critical financial operations remain observable and auditable.

# Referral Philosophy

Referral rewards are not marketing expenses.

They represent long-term partnership incentives.

Referral commissions are automatically calculated, recorded and made available for withdrawal according to business rules.

---

# Long-Term Vision

ATH is designed as an exchange-independent execution platform.

Today it may connect to Nobitex.

Tomorrow it may connect to Binance, KuCoin, Bybit or any compatible exchange.

Exchange connectivity is considered an infrastructure component—not part of the business core.

---

# Relationship with SmartTahlil

ATH intentionally separates market analysis from trade execution.

SmartTahlil generates trading intelligence.

ATH executes trading operations.

A dedicated integration layer will connect both systems in the future.

This separation preserves modularity and allows each platform to evolve independently.

---

# Out of Scope

The following are intentionally excluded from the initial architecture:

- Market analysis engine
- Signal generation engine
- Portfolio optimization AI
- Copy trading marketplace
- Social trading
- Asset custody

These capabilities may be integrated later through independent modules.

---

# Success Criteria

ATH will be considered successful when:

- Customers retain full ownership of assets.
- Trade execution becomes fully automated.
- Manual operational workload is minimized.
- Customer trust increases.
- New exchanges can be integrated with minimal effort.
- SmartTahlil can connect without architectural changes.

---

# Closing Statement

ARK Trade Hub is more than a software platform.

It is the operational foundation of a new generation of intelligent investment services where strategy, execution and ownership remain clearly separated while automation delivers scalability, transparency and trust.
